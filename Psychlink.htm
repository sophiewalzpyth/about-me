Psychology Capstone Project


Correlation Between Participation in High School Activities and College Academic Success

During Spring 2018, I completed my psychology capstone research. Using data collected from four PSYC 101 classes, I tested my hypothesis (students who participated in extracurricular acitivities in high school would have better grades in a freshman level class). Using SPSS, I analyzed my data to drew conclusions based on the results. In April 2018, I presented my results at the West Virginia University capstone poster session.
